Voter Information Project

As my first-week hackathon project for the MLH fellowship, I'm building a webapp that can help you find out all the information you need to vote in the 2020 General Election.

This is a historic election, and in the midst of a pandemic things seem to be more complicated than ever. This website will:

  • Help you register to vote.
  • Help you check if you're registered.
  • Show you where you can vote, both early and on election day.
  • Locate your 3 nearest USPS boxes to drop off your mail-in ballot.
  • Give you any other useful information you need to know.
